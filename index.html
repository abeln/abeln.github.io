Hello! My name is Abel, and I’m a PhD student at Aarhus University, where I'm a member of the Logic and Semantics group. My advisor is Lars Birkedal.

I work on formal verification of distributed systems. I'm also interested in programming language design, type theory, program analysis, and compilers.

I expect to complete my PhD by July 2023. I am interested in an applied scientist or formal methods engineer position in industry, in the fields of formal verification, programming language design, compilers, type systems, and static analysis. I am affiliated with Aarhus University, but live in Waterloo, Canada. Do get in touch if you know of any opportunities in these areas!
